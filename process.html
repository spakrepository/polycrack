The Process

The entire Polycrack process contains the following sequences and adequate care has been taken to ensure that every sequential step is safe and environment friendly.

1)

In chamber vaporization

2)

Polymeric vapour catalysis of the raw material/ feed

3)

Non-condensed gas handling, storage and distribution

4)

Condensed liquid collection

5)

Oil-water separation

6)

Water filtration and re-use, if available

7)

Combustion sequence and combustion of gas inside the reactor shell

8)

Combustion product cleaning

9)

Heat recovery from the combustion products

10)

Carbon residue handling

11)

Cooling water circulation and treatment

12)

Instrumentation, Automation and metering
